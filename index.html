Welcome

Welcome to Euro Payment Systems Ltd. We are specialists in Cash Handling, providing:

  • Coin/ Note Validators
  • Coin Hoppers
  • Ticket Printers
  • Interface Kits
Euro Payment Systems are the sole distributor of Evona Roulette and gaming machines for the UK and Ireland. These include:

  • 8/6/4 player automatic roulettes
  • Bet X sports betting terminals & Bet X Dog and horse racing FOBT machines
  • Arcade management systems
We also provide the following consultancy services to ensure you have a product tailored to your needs:

  • Design Services
  • Machine Testing
  • Fraud Prevention
  • Security
  • Performance analysis
Many years in the payment industry has taught us that the most important part of any gaming or vending machine is the handling of the payment and change. It’s no use having the best machine in the world if the customer can’t get their payment accepted or worse still if the payment jams the machine and puts it out of action!
At Euro Payment Systems Ltd. we are not tied to one supplier. Our aim is simple; to provide the best value systems to suit the budget available. We back this up with support services and even machine design facilities where necessary. Our consultancy services allow you to concentrate on your design while leaving the payment side to the experts.
